Creative Blueprint
Note: Kindly refer the schedule before registering events
DESCRIPTION

Step into the world of imagination and design at Blueprints of Innovation using Figma, where creativity meets strategy. This one-of-a-kind event invites architects, designers, and dreamers to explore the art of blueprinting ideas that shape our future. Whether you're a professional in the industry or an aspiring innovator, this immersive experience will open new doors to your creative potential

RULES
  1. Individual participation only (No teams).
  2. Only Figma is allowed.
  3. No pre-made templates or external UI kits allowed.
  4. The design topic will be given at the start.
  5. Designs should focus on innovation, usability, and aesthetics.
  6. Participant should follow the given theme.
