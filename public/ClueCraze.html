ClueCraze
Note: Kindly refer the schedule before registering events
DESCRIPTION

Teams of 4 race against time to solve ciphers, logic puzzles, and general knowledge clues to find the hidden treasure. Only the first team to reach it wins, with tie-breakers decided through an extra round. Let the hunt begin!

RULES
  1. Each team must have 4 members.
  2. Solve a series of clues, puzzles, and mini-challenges based on ciphers, logic, and general knowledge to reach the treasure.
  3. All team members must stay together; no outside help allowed.
  4. The event is time-bound; only the team that finds the treasure wins.
  5. In case of a tie, an extra round will be conducted to declare the winner.
