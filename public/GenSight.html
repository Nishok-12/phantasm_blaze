GenSight
Note: Kindly refer the schedule before registering events
DESCRIPTION

Test your memory, creativity, and AI prompting skills in GenSight! Participants will be shown an image for a brief period, then must use any AI image generator to recreate the image as accurately as possible from memory. The closer your AI-generated image is to the original, the higher your score!

RULES
    1. Each participant will be shown an image for 30 seconds.
    2. After viewing, participants must prompt an AI image generator to recreate the image as accurately as possible.
    3. Only the AI-generated image output will be judged. No direct submission of the original image or hand-drawn copies is allowed.
    4. Participants can use any AI image generation tool of their choice.
    5. Scoring will be based on accuracy, creativity, and similarity to the original image.
    6. Time limit will be strictly enforced for each round.
