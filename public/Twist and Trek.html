Twist and Trek
Note: Kindly refer the schedule before registering events
DESCRIPTION

Step into the ultimate test of brains and luck! Teams of 4 navigate a snake-and-ladder style board where one member moves, and the other three solve CS puzzles to help them advance. Face surprises, setbacks, and head-to-head battles with rival teams. Reach the finish line first—or cover the most ground before time runs out—to claim victory!

RULES
  1. Each team has 4 members – 1 Mover who navigates the board, and 3 Solvers who solve CS related puzzles.
  2. The Mover moves like a token in a snake-and-ladder game. The number of steps depends on dice roll + puzzle-solving success by the Solvers.
  3. Solvers must complete puzzles within a time limit to allow the Mover to advance.
  4. If two Movers land on the same spot, they face a head-to-head logical battle. The Loser moves back.
  5. Teams may move forward or backward depending on luck (snakes/ladders) and puzzle results.
  6. The game is time-bound; the team that reaches the finish line first wins, and if time runs out, the team that has advanced the farthest on the board is declared the winner.
  7. Teams cannot assist other teams, and only Solvers can solve puzzles for their own Mover.
