CodeVortex
Note: Kindly refer the schedule before registering events
DESCRIPTION

Step into the CodeVortex, where coding meets creativity and strategy! Participants will face a mix of intense programming tasks, brain-teasing puzzles, and fun surprise challenges. Only those with sharp logic, quick thinking, and adaptability can navigate the vortex and emerge victorious.

RULES
    1. Participants will tackle coding problems, puzzles, and mini-challenges throughout the event.
    2. Tasks may range from algorithmic coding to creative or logic-based fun challenges.
    3. Scores will be based on accuracy, speed, and creativity where applicable.
    4. Multiple rounds may be conducted, each with increasing difficulty and surprise tasks.
