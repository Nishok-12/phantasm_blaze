ABOUT PHANTASM BLAZE

Phantasm Blaze is the annual flagship inter-college symposium hosted by the Department of Computer Science. More than just a competition, it is a high-voltage platform designed to ignite student potential across the region. Featuring cutting-edge technical challenges, innovative seminars, and thrilling non-technical events, Phantasm Blaze cultivates academic excellence, fosters collaboration, and connects participants to the global research community. From intense coding duels to visionary design competitions, join us to showcase talent and celebrate the future of technology.
