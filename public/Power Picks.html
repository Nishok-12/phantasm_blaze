Power Picks
Note: Kindly refer the schedule before registering events
DESCRIPTION

Get ready for an electrifying IPL-style auction, where 10 teams compete to build their dream squad by bidding on top cricket players! Each team will have a fixed budget to strategically acquire players across different categories—batsmen, bowlers, all-rounders, and wicketkeepers.Prize Pool Upto : Rs.1500/-.

RULES
    1. Each team will have a fixed budget of 100 crores.
    2. Teams can have a minimum number of 3 and maximum number of 4 participants.
    3. The auction will follow an open-bid system where teams bid in rounds.
    4. A team captain will be responsible for bidding.
    5. Once a bid remains unmatched and the auctioneer goes for a final call, the player is sold.
    6. Bidding above the budget straight away disqualifies the team.
    7. A team must buy at least 11 players and at most 15.
    8. Unsold players may be auctioned again.
    9. Points will be awarded based on the performance of the player on a specific basis which will be announced at the time of the auction.
    10. The team with the highest cumulative points wins the tournament.
